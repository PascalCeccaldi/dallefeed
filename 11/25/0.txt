

#Konyadakatliamvar is trending on Twitter as people express their outrage over the massacre that took place in the city of Konya. Over 100 people were killed in the massacre, which is being called one of the deadliest in recent years.

People are using the hashtag to express their condolences for the victims and their families, and to call for justice. Some are also calling for international intervention in the city, which has been plagued by violence for years.
