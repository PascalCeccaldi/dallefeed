

#博衣こより1周年

It's been one year since the launch of "Boui", the popular fashion brand that is beloved by many young women.

In celebration of this milestone, the company is holding a special event at its flagship store in Tokyo.

There will be plenty of exclusive items on sale, as well as a chance to meet some of the designers and staff who work behind the scenes.

This is sure to be a fun event for all fashion lovers, so be sure to check it out if you can!
