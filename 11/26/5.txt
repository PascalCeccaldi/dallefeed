

MOUNT

The mount trend has been growing steadily for the past few years, with more and more people looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life and escape to the mountains.

Whether it's for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation, the mountain escape is becoming increasingly popular. And it's not just for winter sports anymore - hiking, camping, and even mountain biking are all popular summer activities in the mountains.

So if you're looking for a break from the everyday grind, consider heading to the mountains for a little fresh air and some stunning scenery.
